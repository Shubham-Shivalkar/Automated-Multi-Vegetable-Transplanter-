## ğŸ§© Major Subsystems Designed

1. **Chassis & Frame**

   - Designed for structural strength and stability

2. **Sapling Pickup System**
   - Automated pickup of saplings from trays
   - Designed to minimize plant damage
   - Supports different sapling sizes

3. **Conveying System**
   - Transfers saplings from pickup to planting zone
   - Maintains plant-to-plant spacing
   - Smooth and controlled motion

4. **Digging (Soil Opening) Mechanism**
   - Opens soil at required depth
   - Designed considering soil interaction and resistance

5. **Sapling Drop Mechanism**
   - Vertical placement of saplings into prepared holes
   - Ensures upright orientation during planting

6. **Soil Covering Mechanism**
   - Covers soil after sapling placement
   - Provides stability to transplanted saplings
